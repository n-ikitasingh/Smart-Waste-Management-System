Inductive Metal Sensing

Inductive proximity sensors detect metallic objects using electromagnetic field disturbances, enabling non-contact metal identification.This stage performs rapid pre-filtering to distinguish metallic waste from non-metallic materials.

Mounting: Positioned ~5 mm above the conveyor surface for optimal sensitivity.
Signal Conditioning: Debounce logic (~10 ms) applied to suppress electrical noise and false triggers.

Limit Switches

Mechanical limit switches act as hardware fail-safes that define the physical boundaries of motion. They prevent over-rotation, motor stall, and structural damage during high-speed sorting operations.
