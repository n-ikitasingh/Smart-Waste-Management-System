Data Collection Strategy

The foundation of our AI's accuracy lies in the quality and diversity of our dataset.

Privacy by Design

All data is anonymized at the edge. We ensure zero PII (Personally Identifiable Information) enters the cloud pipeline.

Consistent Sampling

Collecting a diverse set of metal, paper, and plastic objects to ensure balanced training.

Clean Labeling

Organizing and labeling images accurately so the model learns correct patterns.

Controlled Environment

Images are captured in a stable and repeatable setup to avoid noise and improve classification accuracy.

Model Architecture & Training

Our training pipeline is engineered for high accuracy and low-latency inference on the edge.

CNN Architectures

(Lightweight Vision Models)
We use a compact convolution-based model optimized for quick inference and smooth real-time sorting. Custom lightweight Convolutional Neural Networks optimized for real-time vision on ARM-based hardware.

Training Pipeline

(Structured Training Workflow)
The model is trained using a balanced dataset with augmentation and fine-tuning to improve accuracy. Automated hyperparameter tuning using Bayesian optimization to find the most efficient weight distribution.

Validation Metrics

(Performance Evaluation)
Accuracy, loss curves and confusion matrices are monitored to ensure consistent model behavior across all classes. Rigorous F1-score and confusion matrix analysis to ensure balanced performance across all waste categories.

Deployment Formats

(Edge-Optimized Output)
The trained model is exported in lightweight formats suitable for Raspberry Pi deployment. Quantized ONNX and TensorRT models for maximum throughput and minimal energy consumption.
